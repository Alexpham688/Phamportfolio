About Alex Pham

I was born in Vietnam and came to America when i was around 4 or 5. I grew up in Minneapolis, Minnesota until the age of 22 when I decided to move to Davenport, Iowa. I lived there a little over 6 months, but couldn't take it anymore because where ever you went all you would see is miles and miles of corn. So much corn in fact it seemed endless. Then I moved back to Minneapolis, this was around 2002. I then decided to move to Michigan in the year of 2004 and I have been here since.

I worked as a painter at a steel company in Detroit when I first moved here in 2004 until getting laid off for the 2nd time in 2007. Then I worked as a cook in a thai restaurant getting paid cash for bout a year. I then met this girl at a party and we became good friends. She then brought me into the Nails Industry. I thought it wasn't a good fit with me having irregular breathing problems and all, but times were tough you gotta do what you gotta do. I decided to go back to school early 2016, attended ITT-Tech for electrical engineering, about 8 months later the school had shut down country wide. I then applied for Macomb Community College for Computer Programming. It was then my friend had told me about Grand Circus and how his sister in law went here, graduated and is now successful and is banking. I don't care about being rich, I mean who doesn't want to be rich, but being rich doesn't always mean that you're going to be happy. I just want to be successful, good at what I do and loving it at the same time. Because when you love what you do then it's not really a job.
